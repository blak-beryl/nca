about us

welcome to the best learning program for kids

This center is desirous to provide young children with solid foundation to help them achieve their full potential culturally and academically. We foster a balanced curriculum that incorporates the latest technology and educational research to prepare our children for today's fast paced world. We recognize the formative years in a child's life. We will work with parents, teachers, and relevant community partners to develop our children academically, socially, emotionally, and physically in stimulating and loving family care settings.

  • Company mission statement

    The New Century Learning Academy exists to provide qualitative childcare for all families in the community in a warm, nurturing, loving and educational environment. Our focus is to provide a stimulating early learning and childcare experience which promotes each child's social, emotional, physical and cognitive development.

  • Company philosophy and vision

    a. “Caring and Learning with Smile.”

    b. Vision

    The New Century Learning Academy will be first stop for early learning through collaborative leadership that ensures All children receive high-quality care and learning. All families have the support they need for their children to succeed. All children are equipped for their academic and lifelong success.

  • Company goals

    The New Century Learning Academy aims to achieve the following goals:
    a) To provide a safe, healthy, and recreational environment for preschool children.
    b) To provide opportunities for families to be involved with their children’s education and have access to parenting support and education.
    c) To provide a comfortable learning and recreational environment for children while their parents are at work or in schools.
    d) To provide excellent developmentally appropriate childcare for preschool children with focus on individual child’s age and ability to stimulate social, cognitive, physical, and emotional growth.

  • Company objectives

    i. The New Century Learning Academy will create a curriculum which enables each child to develop new skills at their own ability levels.
    ii. We shall ensure each child have safe, healthy, and conducive environment that will guarantee peace of mind for parents trusting their child in our care.
    iii. We shall provide opportunities for parental involvement in the operations and academics affairs of their children
    iv. We shall provide a state-o-the-arts equipment in a safe, friendly supportive environment for the children.
