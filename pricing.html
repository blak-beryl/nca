Late Pick-Up Policy

It is important to note that:

  • Our Center is licensed for specific hours of operations. Children are allowable in our care between 7.00 am and not later than 5.00 pm. Parents/Guardians will be charged for late pick-up fee of $10 for the first 5 minutes and $1.00 per minute after that. Should a child be left for pick-up as specified above, the Center is obligated by the State regulations to call Family and Children's Services/and the Police.
